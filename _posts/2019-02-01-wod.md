# Full-body Friday

Full-body Friday aims to put a strain on the whole body by combining versatile functional movements and working through the exercises without rest.

- **_Practice_** – in teams of 2 (TC 5)

  - Single-Unders/Double-Unders/Triple-Unders
  - Kettlebell Swings

- **_Skill_** (TC 10)

  - Kettlebell Good Mornings

    > Hold the kettlebell in front of your chest firmly gripping and pressing the handle with your hands from the sides.

- **For time** – in teams of 2 (TC 30)

  - 50 Double-unders
  - 50 Walking Lunges w/ Pec Stick
  - 50 Kettlebell Swings
  - 50 Kettlebell Good Mornings
  - 50 Jumping Pull-ups
  - 50 Knees-to-Elbows
  - 50 Wall Ball Shots
  - 50 Box Jumps
  - 50 Push Presses
  - 50 Burpee Tuck Jumps

  > Split up the reps between each other any way you want. Advanced athletes may scale up to 80 reps of each movement as a team.
