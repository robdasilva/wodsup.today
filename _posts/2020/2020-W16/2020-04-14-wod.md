# Tabata Tuesday

Tabata Tuesday aims to focus on establishing and holding full body tension for short bursts of maximum efforts over an extended period of time.

- **Tabata**

  > Rest for 1 minute between tabatas.

  1. Russian Twist
  2. Push-ups
  3. Sit-ups
  4. High to Low Plank
  5. Leg Lowerings
  6. Plank Hold
  7. Hollow Hold
